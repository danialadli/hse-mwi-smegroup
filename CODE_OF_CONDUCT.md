# Code of Conduct
Our team is dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We believe that open and respectful communication is key to a successful project.

## Our Standards
To maintain a positive environment, we expect all participants to:
- Be respectful and constructive in all communications.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Show empathy towards other community members.

Harassment or unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Let's work together to create a safe and productive space for everyone.
